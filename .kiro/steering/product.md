# Product Overview

Business Blueprint is an AI-powered iOS app that helps users discover personalized business ideas based on their skills, personality, and interests.

## Core Value Proposition

The app transforms the business ideation process into a gamified journey, combining AI-powered recommendations with an interactive island-based progression system inspired by Duolingo.

## Key Features

- **AI Business Generation**: Google Gemini AI generates personalized business ideas based on user profiles
- **Gamified Journey**: Island-based timeline with visual progress tracking through business development stages
- **Smart Onboarding**: Interactive quiz flow to capture skills, personality traits, and interests
- **Progress Tracking**: Dashboard with analytics, daily goals, and milestone management
- **AI Assistant**: Contextual chat interface for guidance and timeline adjustments
- **Notes & Reminders**: Island-specific notes with calendar integration

## Target Users

Aspiring entrepreneurs looking for personalized business ideas and structured guidance through the business development process.
